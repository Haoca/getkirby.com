Title: Sections

----

Intro:

Sections help you to organize form elements in the (glossary: Panel) or provide additional information or functionality. Kirby comes with 5 default section types.

----

Text:

Sections are defined in your (glossary: blueprint text: blueprints) and help you to structure your forms in the best possible way. You can define sections once, and then reuse and extend them in other blueprints.

In addition to the default section types, you can also **(link: docs/reference/plugins/extensions/sections text: create your own custom sections)**.
